The Black Cobra

The king cobra—one of the most venomous snakes on the planet—can literally "stand up" and look a full-grown person in the eye. When confronted, they can lift up to a third of its body off the ground and still move forward to attack. King cobras can reach 18 feet in length, making them the longest of all venomous snakes.

  1. The species is native to Africa
  2. Go to Democratic Republic of Congo
  3. Go to Central Congolian lowland forests
  4. We can find the black cobras in the mid forest under trees and underground
